✅ WHAT THE TASK DOES
================================================================================

The mark_no_shows_task automatically marks appointments as "no-show" when:
  1. Appointment status is "SCHEDULED"
  2. Appointment scheduled_at time has passed the grace period
  3. Not yet marked as no-show (no_show_marked_at is NULL)

For each marked appointment:
  • Status changed: SCHEDULED → NO_SHOW
  • Timestamp recorded: no_show_marked_at = now()
  • Audit log entry created with action: NO_SHOW_AUTO_MARKED
  • Multi-hospital scoping respected
